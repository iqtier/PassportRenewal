Bangladeshi Passport Renewal From Canada

All Bangladeshi nationals need valid Machine-Readable Passport (MRP) to travel abroad. The Bangladesh High Commission Ottawa issues and re-issues MRP to bonafide Bangladeshi nationals following due procedure of the Government of Bangladesh.

Select your catagory

  • Catagory #1--Hand Written Passport

    Applicant who are holding Hand written passport, have to Visit the High Commission Office in Ottawa in person for Picture and Biometrics update in addition to the required documents as detailed below. * Applicants born outside Bangladesh but entitled to obtain a Bangladesh Passport by Descent, have to Visit the High Commission Office in Ottawa in person for Picture and Biometrics update in addition to the required documents as detailed below. For Appointment call +1 (613) 236 0138 then press 0.


  • Category # 2 -- Machine Readable Passport(MRP)

    Applicant who are having MRP already ( Machine Readable Computer written passport) and want to,
    * Get Reissue/Renew service for their passport,
    * Make Correction on their Passport or
    * Make Alteration on their passport such as Marital status/names , emergency contact or any other information need to be altered.
    This category applicants can apply in person or via postal mail by just submitting application form and other required supporting documents as detailed below without necessarily submitting their biometric data or visiting the High Commission. The High Commission strongly recommends mailers to use Canada Post mail. Please note that the guidelines provided in this page are indicative. The High Commission reserves the right to request for any additional document at any stage or call the applicant in person for scrutiny by the Consular Officer before rendering any service.

Documents You Need

  • Category # 2 -- Machine Readable Passport(MRP)

    1. Download This form from the link Application Form
    2. Provide your current Original Bangladesh passport
    3. Provide a photocopy of your first pages of your passport (pages containing personal information only).
    4. Two recently taken photographs of the applicant (size: 45 mm x 35 mm or any passport size)

Application Fees

    For all types of Passport services

  • Regular Service fee CAN $150 (One hundred and Fifty Canadian Dollars only) - (processed in 6-8 weeks)*
  • Express Service fee CAN $300 (Three hundred Canadian Dollars only) - (processed in 4 weeks)*


How to apply

  1. Fill the Form

      Category # 2 -- Machine Readable Passport(MRP)

    • Please DO NOT forget to sign your application and write payment details on part provided on your application.

    • Name of Division will be Bangladesh High Commission, Ottawa

    • In the section "Type of delivery" or "পাসপোর্ট বিতরনের প্রকৃতি", you have to seclect what kind of delivery you want. if you select "Regular - সাধারণ", you have to pay 150CAD and your process time will be 6-8 weeks.
      Or if you select "Express - জরুরি", you have to pay 300CAD and your process time will be in less than 4 weeks.

    • USD and CAD are the same. Delete USD and Write CAD,

    • Bank/Mission is the Institution you may order a Bank Draft from, (TD Bank ,RBC, CIBC, etc...)

    • Branch is the branch name or number where the draft is prepared,

    • All information to fill under payment of fee section of the form will be available on your Bank/postal Draft,

    • In the section "Received passport's details"or "গৃহীত পাসপোর্টের তথ্য", you have to fill up with your current or expiring passports information from main page of your passport

    • In the section "Expected Correction", if you are only applying for passpoert renewal , you can just write "Passport renew" in any row in any column. But if you have to make any other corrections, then you have write in exact what you want to change what will be after change.

    • Write your current address and phone number on the top the application for further communication


  2. Make Payment

    • The MRP fee has to be paid by Bank Draft/Pay Order/ Postal order in favor of Bangladesh High Commission, Ottawa, Canada.

    • The applicant can also pay by Credit/ Debit Card in favor of Bangladesh High Commission by being present at the High Commission office in Ottawa.

    • No personal check or cash is accepted.

    N.B: You can do payment from any bank.It's not mandatory for you to have account in the bank.Make sure the payment is in favor to Banglades High Commision, Ottawa , Canada. After make the pay order , dont forget to take the receipt from bank. you will need to send it along with the application.



  3. Gather documents

    • Original Passport- you have to send your original passport(which one is going to be expired) with your application. And don't forget to keep a photocopy of your information page and photocopy of any page with visa.

    • Pay order or bank draft .

    • Photo- You need to take some passport size photo. the size is 45mm x 34mm.If you are a student, you can get this done without any cost from your university library. You only need 2 copies of the picture for this application. And it is better to keep a soft copy of your photograph , so you can use them next time for study permit renewal and TRV renewal, that will save your some time .

    • you need to send a photocopy of your main page of your passport(which contains all your personal information, Page 2 and 3) with this appliction.

    • In Case of any Correction or Alteration on existing MRP, applicant must provide a legal supporting document.

      (For instance, if some one needs to make any change in his/her Marital status, he/she needs to provide copy of supporting Document of Marriage or Divorce Certificate).

    4. N.B: In case of applicant’s failure of submit proper supporting document will delay the processing time.

    The High Commission bears no responsibility for delay or failure of delivery if that mentioned address is wrong, invisible or incomplete.


  4. Shipping Process

    • For shipping your application you can use any courier service ,But for students Canada Post is best option.


    • Application must be send by Xpress envelope, and a paid return Xpress envelop with Applicants full correct address should be sent as well for returning New and Old passport. The High Commission bears no responsibility for delay or failure of delivery if that mentioned address is wrong or incomplete.


    • Envelope size:
      • To Send Your application to the Bangladesh Highcommission:
        • 1 Letter Size envelope(318 x 241 mm)
      • For Bangladesh Highcomission to mail you back your Old and New Passport:
        • 1 Standard size envelope(260 x 159 mm)


    • Write down the tracking number of both envelope to track your mail.


    • Prepeare Your Package:
      • Write your full mailing address on the returning envelope(Standard Size envelope)
      • In the letter size envelope , put:
        • Yourr passport(The one expiring)
        • Your application form
        • Your Pay Order/ Bank Draft copy
        • Photo copy of the main page of your passport
        • Your 2 copies of passport size photo
        • The returning envelope with your proper address on it.

After Apply

Once you have applied for passport renewal, You just have to wait untill you get it back. Wait time depends on your application type. 6 to 8 weeks for regular application and 4 weeks fro Express application. Most of the time you will get your passport back before expected time. You can trac your mail by the tracking number.

Applicants can contact the High Commission if their application has passed Eight weeks for Regular service or Four weeks for Express service since it has been recieved by the High Commission office. During your contact, you will be prompted to provide the following information. Please make sure either of the following information are with you when you contact to High commission:

  • For mailers, Envelope Tracking Number if used,
  • Existing Passport number,
  • First and Last name,
  • And for security purposes, you may be asked for Date of Birth,



Common Mistakes

    Common Mistakes, that Delays your Application,
  • Forget to sign your application with mention of place and date
  • Not providing documents listed above and supporting documents,
  • All fees not be payable to “BANGLADESH HIGH COMMISSION, OTTAWA”.
  • Inappropriate fees /charges or none for the service requested. Please check processing fees above.
  • Forget to send paid express return envelop having correct and full address.
  • During filling-up the form, please type or write by hand in block-letters; illegibility may cause delay or even nullify the process.
  • If you are willing to receive delivery by mail, please use the guaranteed or registered or express mail of Canada Post. Ordinary mail sometimes gets lost or causes unexpected delay. Please also enclose a self-addressed express return envelope of Canada Post. The High Commission bears no responsibility for delay or failure of delivery if that mentioned address is wrong or incomplete.
  • Incomplete form will not be processed and the High Commission will not be held responsible for such applications.
